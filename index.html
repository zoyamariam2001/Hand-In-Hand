About Us

Hand In Hand is a non-profit NGO which aims at reaching & touching the lives of the less fortunate in the society, believing in the slogan-
Sabka Saath Sabka Vikas.
Our team provides various services, free of cost to people belonging to all strata of society, without exception, & also works towards animal care for the stray animals. Another important goal of this NGO is carrying out various activities for a cleaner & greener earth.
Our vision is to live in an advanced & progressive country where everyone has equal opportunity & access to the best education, health and prosperity.

Donate

Being a non-profit organization, we welcome donations in the form of cash, kind & services. You can make a difference by either volunteering your services & time in the various activities carried out by the organization, or donate food, clothes, cash according to your desires & capacity.
Every little contribution will be welcomed with open arms as little drops of water make a mighty ocean.
